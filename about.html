History

How it all started!

Making A Difference Foundation (M.A.D.F.) is an Initiative established on the 27th of March 2020, it was established for the purpose of changing the world into a better place in all aspects of life.

few years!

However, the question boils down to this: What does it really mean to Make A Difference? According to the Board of M.A.D.F., to Make A Difference means “to cause CHANGE” and truly, that is all we have centered our lives on ever since we came together on that blessed day in 2020. How do we cause change? Firstly, we began by orienting ourselves as a team. We did this by inviting motivational speakers, leaders, and profound adults into our midst to lecture us about what it means to head out to the world with the motive of making an impact. The truth is that our age brackets comprise from age 18 to 28 and we are all students currently – most of us are students of the University of Ibadan studying various courses e.g., Law, Geography. Even at Lead City.

currently!

Yes! We did orient ourselves as a team and got to know each other. And of course what is an organization without hierarchy? So we appointed positions. Thereafter, the Covid 19 lockdown came around and we were deprived of the opportunity of meeting each other physically. Yet, we were resilient, we created a social media platform on Whatsapp as commonly used by most Nigerians and we began coordinating sessions on a Whatsapp group, which we named it Making A Difference Foundation Family (MADFF). It has been operative ever since, till date and we have recorded many testimonies of great impact as we reach out to many mentally depressed youths and interacted with them.

Vision

To see that both the young and old minds step out to become the best version of themselves as this also begins with us.

Mission

It is no longer a piece of news that we believe that no matter how little the difference is, it paves way for a better society, our mission is to make the world a better place beginning from our immediate societal background and then to the world at large.
